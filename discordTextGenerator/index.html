Discord Text Generator

Functionality:

This is a small tool I programmed, mainly for myself, to style discord messages without having to type them myself.
This tool takes your input and selected options and gives you your messaged with the added styles. All you have to do is copy it and send it to your favorite servers or friends!

Small note:
Some of the options dont work together. For example, the 'spoiler' option wont work with any of the colours!
